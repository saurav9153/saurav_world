Major Corporate Laws in India

Important Acts Every HR, Payroll and Corporate Professional Should Know

1. Companies Act, 2013

Regulates incorporation, management, governance, directors' duties, audits, financial reporting, mergers, compliance, and winding up of companies in India.

2. Code on Wages, 2019

Provides rules relating to minimum wages, payment of wages, equal remuneration, and timely salary payments for employees.

3. Industrial Relations Code, 2020

Regulates trade unions, industrial disputes, layoffs, retrenchment, standing orders, and dispute resolution between employers and employees.

4. Code on Social Security, 2020

Covers EPF, ESI, gratuity, maternity benefits, employee compensation, gig workers, and other social security provisions.

5. Occupational Safety, Health and Working Conditions Code, 2020

Ensures workplace safety, health standards, welfare facilities, working hours, leave, and occupational safety compliance.

6. Income Tax Act

Governs taxation of individuals and companies, TDS deductions, tax returns, exemptions, and employer tax compliance.

7. Goods and Services Tax (GST)

Regulates indirect taxation on goods and services, GST registration, invoicing, return filing, and tax payments.

8. Payment of Bonus Act

Provides statutory bonus eligibility, calculation methods, minimum and maximum bonus limits for eligible employees.

9. Payment of Gratuity Act

Provides gratuity benefits to eligible employees completing continuous service, subject to statutory provisions and eligibility criteria.

10. Maternity Benefit Act

Protects maternity rights by providing paid leave, nursing benefits, and employment protection for eligible women employees.

11. Sexual Harassment of Women at Workplace Act, 2013 (POSH)

Requires employers to prevent workplace sexual harassment and establish an Internal Committee to address complaints.

12. Shops and Establishments Act

State-specific legislation regulating working hours, holidays, leave, overtime, opening and closing hours, and employment conditions.

13. Factories Act, 1948*

Provides standards for worker safety, health, welfare, working hours, and factory management. (*Many areas are transitioning to the Occupational Safety, Health and Working Conditions Code when brought into force.)

14. Employees' Compensation Act

Provides compensation to employees or dependants for workplace injuries, occupational diseases, disability, or death arising during employment.

15. Apprentices Act

Regulates apprenticeship training, employer obligations, apprentice engagement, contracts, and skill development programmes.
