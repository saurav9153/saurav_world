Your privacy is of paramount importance to us. This Privacy Policy outlines how our mobile application, Calsp (All-in-One Calculator), handles your personal information. We believe in complete transparency and have designed our application to protect your privacy fully.

Core Commitment: Calsp is a non-tracking, static utility application. We do not collect, store, share, or sell any personal data or information from our users.

1. Information Collection and Use

Unlike many modern applications, OmniCalc operates locally on your mobile device. Because of this architecture:

  • No Personal Information: We do not collect any personally identifiable information (PII) such as your name, email address, physical address, phone number, or age.
  • No Financial Data: Although the app features calculators for salary, EPF, and ESI, all calculation data is processed dynamically on your screen. None of your financial figures, salary numbers, or inputs are logged or transmitted anywhere.
  • No Device Data: We do not collect device identifiers, IP addresses, OS versions, or system configurations.

2. No Permissions Required

To operate our standard, scientific, BMI, tensile, and EPF calculators, the app requires zero device permissions. We do not request access to your device's camera, microphone, photo library, location tracking (GPS), or address book.

3. Offline Functionality & Data Security

All calculators in Calsp work completely offline. Because your calculation data never leaves your device, there is no risk of interception or unauthorized transmission. Your calculated figures remain entirely private and are wiped from memory as soon as you close the application.

4. Third-Party Services & Advertisements

Our app does not display advertisements, nor does it utilize any third-party analytics SDKs (such as Google Analytics or Firebase SDKs) that track user behavior across apps and websites. There are no tracking scripts, cookies, or external SDK integrations running within this app.

5. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

Our application is safe for users of all ages, including children under 13. Since we do not collect any user data, personal information, or location identifiers, OmniCalc is fully compliant with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).
