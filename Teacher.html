Teacher Career Roadmap in India

Complete Guide: Primary School Teacher to Professor & Higher Institutions

Introduction

Teaching is one of India's most respected professions. A teacher can work in government schools, private schools, Kendriya Vidyalaya (KV), Navodaya Vidyalaya (JNV), Sainik Schools, Universities, IITs, NITs, IISERs and Central Universities. This roadmap explains every stage from Primary Teacher to Professor.

Career Roadmap

Level 1 - Nursery / Pre-Primary Teacher

  • Qualification: 10+2
  • Course: NTT / D.El.Ed / ECCE
  • Schools: Nursery, Play School, Montessori
  • Age: 18+

Level 2 - Primary Teacher (PRT)

  • Qualification: 12th + D.El.Ed OR Graduation + B.Ed
  • CTET Paper-I or State TET
  • Recruitment:
    • Government Schools
    • Kendriya Vidyalaya (KVS)
    • Navodaya Vidyalaya (NVS)
    • Army Public Schools
    • Private Schools

Level 3 - Upper Primary Teacher (Classes 6-8)

  • Graduation
  • B.Ed
  • CTET Paper-II / State TET
  • Teaching Experience Preferred

Level 4 - Secondary Teacher (TGT)

  • Graduation in Subject
  • B.Ed
  • CTET Paper-II
  • Subject Knowledge
  • KVS / NVS / DSSSB / State Recruitment

Level 5 - Senior Secondary Teacher (PGT)

  • Master Degree (Minimum 50%)
  • B.Ed
  • PGT Recruitment Examination
  • KVS, NVS, State Schools

Level 6 - Assistant Professor

  • Master Degree (55%)
  • UGC NET / CSIR NET
  • OR Ph.D.
  • Universities & Colleges

Level 7 - Associate Professor

  • Ph.D.
  • Research Publications
  • Teaching Experience
  • Academic Performance

Level 8 - Professor

  • Excellent Research
  • Years of Teaching Experience
  • International Publications
  • Academic Leadership

Suggested Career Progression

12th → D.El.Ed / Graduation → B.Ed → CTET/TET → Primary Teacher → TGT → PGT → Master Degree → UGC NET → Assistant Professor → Associate Professor → Professor
