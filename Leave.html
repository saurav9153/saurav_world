Leave & Working Hours

Employees are entitled to various types of leave under Indian labour laws. The Occupational Safety, Health and Working Conditions Code, Shops & Establishments Acts and company policies regulate working hours and leave.

Standard Working Hours

Particular Details
Daily Working Hours 8–9 Hours
Weekly Working Hours 48 Hours Maximum
Weekly Holiday One Day
Rest Interval 30 Minutes after 5 Hours
Overtime Double Rate of Wages

Types of Leave

Leave Description
Casual Leave Personal work or emergency.
Sick Leave Medical illness.
Earned Leave Accumulated leave based on service.
Maternity Leave 26 Weeks (Eligible Women Employees).
Paternity Leave Depends upon employer policy/Government rules.
National Holidays Republic Day, Independence Day, Gandhi Jayanti.
Important:
  • Employees should maintain attendance properly.
  • Leave records must be maintained by HR.
  • Overtime should be paid according to applicable labour laws.
  • Different states may have different Shops & Establishments rules.
