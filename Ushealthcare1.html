Medical Terminologies & Coding Systems

Medical Terminology

Medical terminology is the universal language used by healthcare professionals to describe diseases, procedures, body parts, medications and treatments. Understanding medical terminology helps medical coders and billers accurately interpret medical records.

Term Meaning
Diagnosis Identification of a disease or condition.
Procedure Medical treatment or service performed.
Acute Sudden illness lasting a short period.
Chronic Long-lasting disease.
Benign Non-cancerous.
Malignant Cancerous.
Inpatient Patient admitted to hospital.
Outpatient Patient treated without hospital admission.
Prognosis Expected outcome of disease.
Prescription Medication ordered by physician.

ICD-10-CM

ICD stands for International Classification of Diseases. ICD-10-CM codes identify diseases, symptoms, injuries and medical conditions. These diagnosis codes explain why a patient received healthcare services.

Features

  • Diagnosis Coding System
  • Maintained in the United States for clinical diagnosis reporting
  • Usually 3–7 characters
  • Used by physicians, hospitals and insurance companies
ICD Code Description
E11.9 Type 2 Diabetes Mellitus without complications
I10 Essential Hypertension
J06.9 Upper Respiratory Infection
M54.5 Low Back Pain
R50.9 Fever

CPT Codes

CPT (Current Procedural Terminology) codes describe medical procedures and services performed by healthcare providers. CPT codes answer the question "What service was performed?"

CPT Categories

  • Category I – Standard Medical Procedures
  • Category II – Performance Measurement
  • Category III – Emerging Technologies
CPT Code Description
99213 Established Patient Office Visit
93000 Electrocardiogram (ECG)
80053 Comprehensive Metabolic Panel
71046 Chest X-Ray
36415 Collection of Venous Blood

HCPCS Codes

HCPCS stands for Healthcare Common Procedure Coding System. These codes identify medical equipment, supplies, ambulance services, prosthetics and certain drugs not covered by CPT.

Levels

  • Level I – CPT Codes
  • Level II – Supplies, equipment, ambulance and medications
HCPCS Code Description
A0428 Ambulance Transportation
E0114 Crutches
L3908 Wrist Brace
J1885 Ketorolac Injection
E0601 CPAP Machine

ICD vs CPT vs HCPCS

Feature ICD-10-CM CPT HCPCS
Purpose Diagnosis Procedure Supplies & Equipment
Question Answered Why? What Service? What Supply?
Code Format Alphanumeric 5 Digits Letter + Numbers
Used By Providers & Payers Physicians Medicare & Others
Easy Memory Trick
  • ICD → Why was the patient treated?
  • CPT → What procedure was performed?
  • HCPCS → Which supplies, equipment or drugs were used?
